 A red eye is usually nothing to worry about and often gets better on its own. But sometimes it can be serious and you'll need to get medical help.

What to do if you have a red eye
 What to do if you have a red eye If your eye does not hurt and your sight is not affected, it's probably nothing serious.  Non-urgent advice: See a GP if: you have a red eye for more than a few daysyour child is under 2 years old and has a red eyeIf your GP cannot find what's causing your red eye, they may refer you to an eye specialist (ophthalmologist) for tests.
